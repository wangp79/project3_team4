BUBBLE CHART

Understanding The Chart

The Bubble Chart below depicts those NEOs that will get the closest to Earth in the next 50 years.

The X-Axis depicts how close the objects get to Earth and the size of the circles represents the Magnitude of each object.

For reference, the distance of the moon from the Earth is 0.002569 AU or around 384,399 km

To Zoom: Scroll Mouse button. To drag: Click left button
